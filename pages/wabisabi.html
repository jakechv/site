wabisabi

A world view centered on the acceptance of transience and imperfection.Often associated with minimalism, it's the art of appreciating beautythat is 'imperfect. impermanent and incomplete' - as derived from thethree marks of existence.

Asymmetry, roughness, austerity, intimacy and modesty are all importantcharacteristics of the philosophy.
