  2. Start a particular idea

Unfortunately, I continue to stagnate before steps 3 or 4. I'm not quitesure why, but I sense it's that I'm often either scared to execute (thatmy skills may not match my reading) or that I'm scared to publish whatI've executed (afraid of judgement). Some things have fallen to thewayside after losing some time, but most I have become scared of andtaken too long to do.
